## Common & Recommended Rules / Guidelines

| Principle / Rule | Purpose / Rationale | Sample Wording / Tips | Link |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Confidentiality / Privacy** | Creates safe container; encourages openness | “What is said in the group stays in the group.” | [mankindprojectuki.org](https://mankindprojectuki.org/mens-group-ground-rules?utm_source=chatgpt.com) |
| **Speak from “I” / Personal Experience** | Reduces projection; encourages ownership | Use “I feel / I experienced” instead of “you always…” | [digitalcollections.library.gsu.edu](https://digitalcollections.library.gsu.edu/digital/api/collection/gsv/id/837/download?utm_source=chatgpt.com) |
| **No unsolicited advice / no “shoulds”** | Prevents dominance, judgment | Share what has worked for you, not prescriptions | [mensgroup.com](https://mensgroup.com/start-mens-group/?utm_source=chatgpt.com) |
| **Right to pass / optional sharing** | Respects boundaries | “I can pass; I’m not obligated to share beyond comfort” | [digitalcollections.library.gsu.edu](https://digitalcollections.library.gsu.edu/digital/api/collection/gsv/id/837/download?utm_source=chatgpt.com) |
| **Punctuality / start & end on time** | Respects time; keeps structure | “We begin and end circle on time.” | [mankindprojectuki.org](https://mankindprojectuki.org/mens-group-ground-rules?utm_source=chatgpt.com) |
| **Being present / stay “here & now”** | Encourages immediacy | “Be here now” — focus on present feelings | [digitalcollections.library.gsu.edu](https://digitalcollections.library.gsu.edu/digital/api/collection/gsv/id/837/download?utm_source=chatgpt.com) |
| **Take responsibility / ownership** | Encourages participation, accountability | “You support the culture, maintain trust, take initiative” | [mensgroupmanual.com](https://mensgroupmanual.com/page35.html?utm_source=chatgpt.com) |
| **Nonviolence / respectful speech** | Prevents harm; ensures safety | No verbal attacks, ridicule, passive aggression | [mankindprojectuki.org](https://mankindprojectuki.org/mens-group-ground-rules?utm_source=chatgpt.com) |
| **No dominating / share airtime** | Ensures equal voices | Moderator may intervene to balance airtime | [mensgroup.com](https://mensgroup.com/start-mens-group/?utm_source=chatgpt.com) |
| **Agreement on group boundaries & logistics** | Clarifies expectations | E.g. phones off, no intoxication, notify absences | [mensgroupmanual.com](https://mensgroupmanual.com/page35.html?utm_source=chatgpt.com) |
| **Consequences / accountability** | Maintains integrity, safety | Define steps for rule violations | [icanotes.com](https://www.icanotes.com/2024/02/20/establishing-effective-ground-rules-for-successful-group-therapy/?utm_source=chatgpt.com) |
