# Safety

> We are responsible for the environment we create together.

## For our group this means

We commit to fostering an environment of trust and openness. We acknowledge that our words and actions have an impact on those around us, and we take responsibility for that.

Where we get it wrong — and we will — we remain open to feedback from our brothers. We don't dig in. We don't deflect. We listen, and we commit to learning how to do better.

Safety here doesn't mean comfortable. Discomfort is part of the work. It means no one is humiliated. No one is bullied. No one leaves feeling smaller than when they arrived.

## Exceptions

Physical safety is non-negotiable. Threatening or violent behaviour ends the meeting. Full stop.
