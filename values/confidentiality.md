# Confidentiality

> What happens in the group stays in the group.

Members are free to speak about their own experience outside the group. They are not free to speak about anyone else's.

This is the foundation. Without it, nothing else works.

## For our group this means

What is said here stays here. You may take your own story into the world — that belongs to you. What others share belongs to them, and stays in this room.

## Exceptions

Every tradition that takes this seriously also names the limits. Confidentiality is not a shield for harm. If someone is at risk — to themselves or others — that takes precedence. We name this not to create fear, but to be honest: trust is built on clarity, not on promises we can't keep.
